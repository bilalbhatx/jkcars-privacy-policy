Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 21 October 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to JK Cars — a mobile application that helps users buy and sell cars and bikes in Jammu & Kashmir. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to improve your experience and provide app functionality:

a) Personal Information

  • Name
  • Contact Number
  • Email Address (if provided)
  • Profile or Dealer Information (showroom name, address, etc.)

b) Listing Information

  • Vehicle details (brand, model, price, year, etc.)
  • Uploaded images of vehicles
  • City or location (selected or detected)

c) Device & Technical Information

  • Device model and operating system
  • App version
  • Anonymous usage and crash analytics data

d) Location Data

If you grant location permission, we use it only to detect your approximate city or district to improve the accuracy of listings. You can disable location access anytime from your device settings.

3. How We Use the Information

  • To display and manage your vehicle listings
  • To improve app functionality and performance
  • To show nearby vehicles based on your location
  • To prevent fraud, spam, or misuse
  • To communicate updates and provide support

We never sell or rent your personal data to any third party.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers. Images are uploaded to Firebase Storage and other listing data is saved in Firestore. We implement access control and encryption wherever possible to protect your data from unauthorized access.

5. Permissions We Use

Permission Purpose Required?
Location To detect your city or district automatically Optional
Camera To take photos of vehicles for listings Optional
Storage / Photos To select and upload vehicle images Optional
Internet To connect to Firebase and fetch data Required

6. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services that follow their own privacy and data policies:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Storage, Analytics)
  • Google AdMob (Ads and Monetization)

7. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not directed toward children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from minors.

8. Your Rights

  • You can access or edit your listings anytime.
  • You can request deletion of your account or data by contacting us.
  • You can manage permissions (camera, location, storage) from your phone’s settings.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or data-related requests, please contact us:

Email: support@jkcars.in
Location: Srinagar, Jammu & Kashmir, India

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect new features or legal requirements. Updates will be posted here with a new “Last Updated” date.
